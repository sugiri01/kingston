Job Details

Edathena is hiring for the role of Junior Campus Ambassador!

Responsibilities of the Candidate:

Campus Outreach:

  • Organize and conduct engaging workshops, presentations, and events on campus to promote Edathena's programs and services.
  • Whatsapp Marketing using your networks.
  • Build relationships with student organizations, clubs, and societies to increase awareness and participation in Edathena initiatives.
  • Collaborate with the marketing team to develop and implement effective campus marketing strategies.

Student Engagement:

  • Interact with students on a regular basis to understand their needs, aspirations, and challenges.
  • Provide guidance and support to students interested in pursuing higher education or career opportunities with Edathena.
  • Organize and participate in career fairs, information sessions, and other events to connect students with potential employers.

Content Creation:

  • Assist in creating engaging and informative content for social media, website, and other marketing channels.
  • Share personal experiences and insights to inspire and motivate fellow students.
  • Contribute to the development of case studies, testimonials, and other marketing materials.

Data Collection and Analysis:

  • Collect and analyze data on student engagement, program effectiveness, and marketing campaign performance.
  • Prepare reports and presentations to share insights with the team and stakeholders.
  • Use data to identify areas for improvement and optimize campus outreach strategies.

Requirements:

Excellent Communication Skills:

  • Ability to articulate ideas clearly and concisely in both written and verbal communication.
  • Strong interpersonal skills and the ability to build rapport with diverse groups of students.
  • Experience in public speaking and presentation delivery.

Leadership and Initiative:

  • Proven ability to take initiative and work independently.
  • Strong leadership qualities and the ability to motivate and inspire others.
  • Experience in organizing and leading events and activities.

Marketing and Social Media Savvy:

  • Understanding of marketing principles and best practices.
  • Proficiency in using social media platforms to reach and engage target audiences.
  • Experience in creating and managing social media content.

Technical Skills:

  • Proficiency in using Microsoft Office Suite and Google Workspace applications.
  • Basic knowledge of data analysis tools and techniques.
